⚖️ DMCA Policy

Copyright protection — hum seriously lete hain

Last Updated: January 2025

Hamari Policy

Lul Downloader copyright laws ka respect karta hai. Hum Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) ka compliance karte hain. Agar aapka content hamari service ke through improperly access ho raha hai, toh report karo.

DMCA Takedown Request Kaise File Karo?

DMCA takedown notice submit karne ke liye, neeche di gayi information ke saath humse contact karo:

  • Aapka naam aur contact information
  • Copyright ki description — kaunsa content aapka hai?
  • Infringing content ki location — URL ya description
  • Good faith statement: "I have a good faith belief that use of the copyrighted materials described above is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law."
  • Accuracy statement: "I swear, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notification is accurate and that I am the copyright owner or am authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed."
  • Electronic signature

Contact for DMCA

DMCA requests ke liye contact form use karo aur subject mein "DMCA Request" likho. Hum 48-72 ghante mein respond karte hain.

Counter Notice

Agar aapka content galti se remove kiya gaya hai, toh counter-DMCA notice file karo. Usme bhi same information chahiye hogi plus ek statement ki aap good faith mein believe karte hain ki content removed by mistake tha.

Repeat Infringers

Hum repeat infringers ki access block kar dete hain.
